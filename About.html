ABOUT US

We are Matías Alcalá, Pedro Torres, and Antonio Valderrábano, three 16-year-old friends who decided to come together to create this project. Since a young age, we have been interested in the world of sneakers and have spent hours researching and learning about their history and evolution. A group of teenagers who share a love for sneakers have come together to create a website where they can share their knowledge and passion with others who share their interests. Their goal is to provide helpful information to help people make informed decisions when purchasing sneakers, as well as to foster a community where sneaker enthusiasts can connect with one another.

We love discovering new trends and styles, and we are always keeping an eye out for the latest releases on the market. But we don't just focus on the aesthetic aspects of sneakers, we also enjoy learning about their technical features and performance. With this website, we want to share our experience and knowledge with other sneaker enthusiasts, and provide valuable information to help them make informed decisions when purchasing a pair of sneakers. Additionally, we want to create an online community where sneaker lovers can connect and share their interests and passion. Thank you for visiting us, and we hope you enjoy our content.
